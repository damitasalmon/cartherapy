About the Series

Car convos are some of the best therapy sessions money can't buy.
Car Therapy is a digital comedy series about a down-on-her-luck college therapist who decides to drive for a ride sharing app to make ends meet during a pandemic. On her rides, she is exposed to new people and new parts of LA and always ends up dishing advice, whether solicited or not.

Meet the Creator

Jennelle Williams is a storyteller at heart, who became a multi-hyphenate (writer/director/producer, and sometimes actor!) to get her stories told. She took the leap to LA after studying film in her hometown of DC, driving across the country for her dreams. Since moving to sunny California, she has worked on several sets, starting out as a PA and working her way up to staff writer on two sitcoms. She plans on taking her own projects to the big screens; writing and developing half-hour shows and feature length films focusing on multi-generational familial, platonic, and romantic relationships from a black woman's point of view.

Cast

Christian Krauspe
as "Brian"

Christian Krauspe is an Illinois native, coming from just | outside the Windy City. As a graduate student, he earned his MFA in Playwriting from Arizona State, then moved to Los Angeles. His work has been performed all over the US. When not stuck at home because of a pandemic, Christian's main performance passion is Improv, and has most recently played at UCB and Second City. He really, really misses it.

Karisa Tate
as "Amy"

Karisa Tate is a Los Angeles-based actor working in television, film and new media. She is a graduate of USC with a degree in Theatre and Cinematic Arts. Recently, Karisa wrote, starred in, and executive produced the new, dark comedy series, RxR. It's in the final stages of post-production and will be entering the festival season next year. Karisa also recently starred in the comedic short, Across the Room, with the team from The Young and the Restless. It's been doing the festival circuit and has picked up several awards, including Best Short at the Portland Film Festival. Prior to Across the Room, Karisa acted in the quirky film Apuelogy, which won best comedy at the Austin Short Film Festival.

Cecelia Friday
as "Chrissy"

Cecelia Friday began her relationship with the arts at the age of 6, with her classical dance classes and elite theater camps. She's still honing her relationship as an adult, acting and dancing with some of the greats. Prior to moving to Los Angeles, Cecelia danced and acted in two national touring plays alongside Lynn Whitfield, Robin Givens and Clifton Powell, to name a few. Since her move to LA, her talents have been more geared towards film and television where she currently has a running Uber Eats commercial and has done several movies including “The Man In 3B.” Even though Cecelia is not afraid to bring out the drama she has been told in several casting workshops that she is the "girl next door" type, but with great comedic timing and pacing. Cecelia has plans on becoming a series regular on a sitcom in the near future or any feature alongside someone like Angela Bassett. Off screen Cecelia claims that her biggest accomplishment is being a mom to her beautiful 3 year old daughter Celine.

Bileko Wissa
as "Christoph"

Bileko Aziza Wissa Jr, is a Congolese-American stand up comedian and actor from LA. He's been on shows like “Laugh with Africa!” on Amazon Prime and sketches on Jimmy Kimmel Live! He is currently a part of a Congolese podcast and Comedy Group called “The Bantu Boyz” where we highlight Congolese culture through comedy and sketch while interviewing | talent across the diaspora. With a background in Biomedical Sciences and Public Health, he understands the importance of the therapy both genetically and psychologically. He's looking forward to being a part of the Car Therapy series and working with talented director Jennelle Williams.

Mina Q
as "T"

Mina Q- Not your average comedic talent, Mina Q. has pushed against the boundaries of societal norms and brought them to the stage and internet. Originally from Atlanta, Mina's move to Los Angeles immediately fueled her mission to transform all of her losses into laughs. From her self produced online content to her appearances on Tru TV's "Laff Mobb's Laff Tracks" and FoxSoul's "Tammi Mac Late Show," she's been hard at work creating an undoubtably diverse comedic arsenal.

Jasmyn Carter
as "La'Daria"

Jasmyn Carter is a young stand-up comedian from Cleveland, Ohio. She's toured improvs and various comedy clubs across the country, opening for legends such as John Witherspoon, Tommy Davidson, and many more. After taking her unique brand of comedy to the internet in 2013, she has gained more than 100,000 followers on Instagram from her original comedic sketches. Her dedication and creativity on and off stage are nothing short of a legend in the making.

Tony Baker
as "Hoodie"

Chicago native, Stand-Up Comedian/Actor Tony Baker has been making people laugh for nearly a decade, blessing stages around the world with his unique brand of humor and style. In addition to being seen performing at the hottest comedy clubs around the country and organically making millions of people laugh worldwide with his legendary viral voiceover videos on social media, Tony Baker’s comedic skills has landed him on the big screen and television: finalist on NBC’s The Last Comic Standing (Season 8 & 9 ), NBC’s The Carmichael Show (Season 2), HBO’s All DEF Comedy (Season 1), Trutv's Standup/storytelling showcase, Laff Mobb’s Laff Tracks, the Academy Award nominated movie Whiplash and his many stand-up comedy specials.

Jonnae Thompson
as "Breanna"

Jonnae Thompson is a stand-up comedian, actress, writer and vocalist born and raised in San Diego, California. You may recognize her from Tru TV’s “Laff Tracks” and Season 1 of Revolt TV’s “Funny AF," as well as internet platforms such as “All Def”. According to Jonnae, ordinary life is full of humor. From socio-economic conditions to body image obsession, she speaks from personal experience about real issues faced by real people. Her ability to find the intersectionality we share across the globe is a breath of fresh air.

Ash Saunders
as "Nikki"

Ash Saunders, a born and raised Long Islander, has always been super creative and active. It is the reason why she chose to pursue the entertainment industry as a child and furthered her study of the craft at Hampton University. She received her Bachelor in Performance Art, and from there, she landed an internship in Los Angeles- and the rest is history. The deeper she journeyed into the industry, the deeper her love grew working not only on camera but behind the scenes as well. Aside from acting, writing, and directing, she also enjoys DIY projects, cooking, and fashion design. Her life’s motto is, "It’s either a lesson or a blessing. Remember perspective is key". Ash’s hope for herself is to continue making|strides within the entertainment industry, and experiencing all that life has to offer.
